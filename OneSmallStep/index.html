Cosmic Curiosities

🌕

Synchronous Rotation

The Moon rotates on its axis at the same rate that it orbits the Earth, which is why we always see the same side.

👣

Footprints Forever

Without wind or water to erode them, the footprints left by Apollo astronauts will likely last for millions of years.

🚀

Computing Power

The smartphone in your pocket has over 100,000 times more processing power than the computer that guided Apollo 11 to the Moon.

🌍

Moon Quakes

Just like Earth, the Moon has quakes. Caused by the gravitational pull of Earth, they can last up to an hour.
